    Frequently Asked Questions

    Does AXA Mansard cover payment defaults?

    +

    No, we do not cover payment default. Our credit life insurance products are designed to pay off a borrower’s outstanding debts in the event the borrower falls critically ill, permanently disabled, loses his/her job or dies.

    Does insurance start once the loan schedule has been received?

    +

    No, the insurance rule ‘No Premium, No Cover’ applies. Once premium has been received, cover is initiated taking into cognizance on the loan disbursement date. The terms of the business relationship contained in the signed Service Level Agreement (SLA) that is to be abided by.

    Is credit insurance suitable for SMEs?

    +

    Yes, it certainly is. Many of our credit life insurance products are suitable for both individuals, SMEs and large companies. We have even bundled our credit life insurance with Fire and Special peril cover + Burglary (optional) to protect the goods of debtors against damage from fire or theft whilst coving the life of the business owner or the highest stake holder of the business.

    What happens if debtors are unable to fulfil their loan obligations?

    +

    When a debtor defaults on loan, owing to their death, illness, disability or job loss as contained in the policy document, kindly notify us immediately of the event and support the claim with substantiating documents stated in the policy document/ Service Level Agreement (SLA). Once received, our claims officers will assess the documents or inspect and evaluate the damaged property after which we submit our settlement offer once approved.

    How long does claims take to be settled?

    +

    We pay all valid insurance claims submitted under the policy within Seventy-Two (72) hours for (for Life covers) and Four to Five Days and Fire and Special peril/Burglary claims after the receipt of substantiating documents stated in the Service Level Agreement (SLA).
